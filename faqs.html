Peek FAQs

How do I get a 'Peek Pick'?

"Peek Picks" are posts featured by Peek. Peek monitors the service round the clock and handpicks posts based on their creativity.

Can I share Peeks to other social networks?

The technology behind Peek only lives within the Peek app, so currently, no. You can invite friends to join Peek via this website or direct from the App Store.

How do I save Peeks to my phone?

When you take a Peek, each photo is saved automatically into your camera roll (separately, not as a Peek). We are working on adding an option to Settings so in future you choose to turn this feature on/off.

Why aren't the # peeks on my post increasing?

The # peeks will increase when other users peek at your post. But it will not increase if you peek at your own post.

How do I delete my Peek account?

If you want to delete your account, please send your username to support@joinpeek.com and we'll delete it for you.

How do I reset my password?

We're building a password recovery feature into the next version of Peek. In the meantime, please send your username to support@joinpeek.com and we'll reset it for you.

How can I report an inappropriate post?

Click the "3-dot" menu button located under each Peek, then select "Report this post". We will review within 24hrs and delete the post if it violates the Terms of Service.

When is Peek coming to Android?

Android is high on our priority list. We don't have a specific release date yet, but will let you know as soon as we do.
